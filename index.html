Anti-Harassment Policy

Effective January 2025 · All employees

On this page

  • Our Commitment
  • Prohibited Conduct
  • Reporting
  • Consequences

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a work environment free from harassment, discrimination, and retaliation. All employees, contractors, and visitors are expected to treat each other with respect and dignity.

Prohibited Conduct

Harassment based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic is prohibited. This includes unwelcome conduct such as offensive comments, jokes, gestures, or physical conduct that creates a hostile or intimidating work environment.

Reporting

If you experience or witness harassment, report it to your manager, HR, or through the anonymous reporting line. Reports will be investigated promptly and confidentially to the extent possible. Retaliation against anyone who reports in good faith is prohibited.

Consequences

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination. We take all reports seriously and are committed to addressing them fairly and consistently.
